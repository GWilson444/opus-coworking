Welcome to OPUS

Everytown's Premier Office Workspace

OPUS is Everytown's newest workspace, designed to foster great work and build community

Whether you're a founder, freelancer, or running a team, OPUS has flexible options to foster your productivity and help you to excel!

If you are looking for a beautiful, flexible space to power out work for a day, or a new longterm HQ for your startup, OPUS has 24/7 options for private offices, dedicated desks, and open seating.

To keep you and the community safe, OPUS has over 30 health measures in place, including daily Health Check monitoring and touchless temperature taking, nanocrystal self-sanitizing wraps on high-touch surfaces, extensive air filtration, and touchless entry and transactions.

To learn more, please click the Book a Tour button located at the bottom right of the page, or Contact Us with your questions.
